The Helix72 TAM Structural Analyzer is a real-time biomechanical analysis system designed to evaluate human posture, motion, and structural stability through a unified framework combining the Tri-Antagonist Matrix™ (TAM) and the Helix72 stability engine.

This system translates human movement into measurable vector-based outputs, enabling clinicians and operators to identify structural imbalances, compensation patterns, and stability states with clarity and consistency.

Built for clinical and performance environments, Helix72 provides actionable insight for:

Chiropractic assessment

Massage therapy planning

Physical therapy evaluation

Sports rehabilitation and performance analysis
